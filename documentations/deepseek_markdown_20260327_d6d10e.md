# StepIn: Accelerating Integration Through Daily News

**Understanding your new home — from day one.**

---

## The Problem: Integration Takes Too Long

**302,199** immigrants arrive in the Netherlands annually. Yet:
- **2+ years** wait before formal integration begins
- **73%** of status holders have unknown education level
- **Too many** lack sustainable employment *(State Secretary Nobel, 2026)*
- Municipalities spend **€2,500–4,000** per status holder on interpretation annually

The 2026 interim evaluation confirms: *waiting times are too long* and *combining work with language lessons remains difficult*.

---

## The Insight: Language = Civic Participation

Research shows:
- **Higher language proficiency directly increases civic engagement** among immigrants *(Boyd, 2012)*
- Host-country news consumption improves political knowledge and language skills *(Dalisay, 2012)*
- Native-language media helps immigrants navigate host society *(Zhou & Cai, 2002)*
- **Balanced use of both leads to better outcomes** *(Reis, 2010; Mao, 2015)*

**The gap:** No tool exists that combines both simultaneously.

---

## Our Solution: StepIn

A dual-mode news platform available **24/7**, **immediately**, at **zero cost to municipalities**.

| Mode | What It Does |
|------|--------------|
| **Native Language** | Dutch news translated into 9 languages (Turkish, Arabic, Polish, Ukrainian, French, Farsi, Italian, Romanian, Bulgarian) |
| **Dutch Learner** | Simplified Dutch (A2/B1/B2) + vocabulary translations |

**User flow:** Access → Understanding → Language Acquisition → Integration
